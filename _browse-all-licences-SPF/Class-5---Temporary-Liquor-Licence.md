## Apply for Class 5 - Temporary Liquor Licence

Singapore Police Force (SPF)

### Application Details

<p>Under the Liquor Control (Supply and Consumption) Act 2015, any supply of liquor in Singapore will require a liquor licence issued by the Licensing Officer. "Liquor" is defined as a beverage and or substance, containing more than 0.5% ethanol by mass or volume.</p>

### Documents Needed

<p>Hawker Licence<br />Only required for Class 2B Licence applications.</p>
<p>Registration of Society Certificate<br />Only applicable for Non-ACRA entity</p>
<p>URA planning permission / HDB lodgement and supporting letter<br />Supporting letter from HDB Lodgement or change of Use permission from URA.</p>
<p>ACRA Business Profile<br />ACRA Business Profile with company details</p>

### Requirements

<p>Limited Liability Partnership<br />Applicant must be having position:<br />* PARTNER</p>
<p>Company<br />Applicant must be having one of the following position:<br />* MANAGING DIRECTOR<br />* SECRETARY<br />* DIRECTOR<br />* CHIEF EXECUTIVE OFFICER</p>
<p>Business (Sole Proprietor/<br />Partnership)<br />Applicant must be having one of the following position:<br />* OWNER<br />* AUTHORIZED REPRESENTATIVE</p>
<p>LP<br />Applicant must be having one of the following position:<br />* GENERAL PARTNER<br />* LIMITED PARTNER</p>
